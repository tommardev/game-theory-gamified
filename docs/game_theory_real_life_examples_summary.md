# Real Life Examples of Game Theory: Summary

Game theory appears in everyday situations where strategic decisions are dependent on the actions of others.

### Key Real-Life Categories
1.  **Business:** Used in pricing wars (matching competitor prices), labor negotiations, and designing high-stakes auctions.
2.  **Everyday Life:** Seen in traffic behavior (the "Chicken" game), managing shared resources (Tragedy of the Commons), and basic social planning.
3.  **Politics & Society:** Influences political campaigning, international treaties, and models like the arms race or climate change agreements (often resembling the Prisoner's Dilemma).

**Core Rule:** Any situation where your optimal choice requires anticipating another's actions is a real-world application of game theory.
